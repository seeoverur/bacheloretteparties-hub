Where to Book Your Airbnb

Navigating Charleston's Neighborhoods for the Perfect Group Stay for Charleston bachelorette Parties

Cannonborough-Elliotborough

This is the "Short-Term Rental Capital" of downtown. While much of the historic district forbids Airbnbs under 30 days, this neighborhood is the exception. It is the gold mine for bachelorette parties.

The Vibe: Walkable, trendy, and packed with local flavor. You’re 3 blocks from the bars of Upper King Street. You’ll find restored wood-fronted houses that have been converted into luxury rental suites.

Folly Beach

If you want the "classic" bachelorette party experience—big houses, beach access, and a laid-back atmosphere—Folly is the place. It's about a 20-minute Uber from downtown.

The Vibe: Surfer-cool and unpretentious. Look for rentals near Center Street so you can walk to bars like Surf Bar or Taco Boy. It's the best spot for groups who want a private pool and ocean views.

Park Circle (North Charleston)

The "Brooklyn" of Charleston. This is the best value for groups who want to save money on the house to spend more on the experiences.

The Vibe: Craft breweries, disc golf, and a killer main street (East Montague Ave). It’s about 15 minutes north of downtown but offers much larger homes for a fraction of the price.

Mount Pleasant / Shem Creek

A sophisticated middle ground. Staying near Shem Creek gives you waterfront dining and easy access to both downtown and the beaches of Sullivan's Island.

The Vibe: Boat charters leave from here daily. It’s perfect for the group that wants to spend Saturday on a fishing boat and Saturday night at a high-end steakhouse.

Pro-Tip: Check the "Occupancy"

Charleston is cracking down on over-crowded rentals. If a listing says "Sleeps 12," make sure it actually has 12 beds, not 4 beds and 4 air mattresses. For the best experience, look for "The Palmetto State Complex" or similar properties that allow you to book multiple units in one footprint. This keeps the group together without violating local fire codes or noise ordinances.
