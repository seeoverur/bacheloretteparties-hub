The Golden Rules of Charleston Beaches

  • No Alcohol on the Sand: This is strictly enforced. Keep it in your rental or stick to the beachfront bars.
  • Parking is War: If you aren't on the sand by 10:30 AM on a Saturday, you aren't parking within a mile of the ocean. Uber is highly recommended.
  • Leave No Trace: Charlestonians are protective of their coastline. Pick up your cans and keep the dunes clean.
