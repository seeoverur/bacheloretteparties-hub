Brunch & Recovery

The best spots to cure a King Street hangover from the Charleston bachelorette parties.

1. Poogan's Porch

An absolute Charleston legend. Located in a restored Victorian house, Poogan's serves the kind of Southern comfort food that was designed for recovery. Their Chicken and Waffles and Shrimp & Grits are world-famous for a reason.

"If you don't order the biscuits with pimento cheese, did you even go to Charleston?"

2. Miller's All Day

This is the "cool kid" of the brunch scene. With a focus on grains (they have an antique grist mill in the front window), itâ€™s a modern take on the diner. For bachelorette parties, the B.E.C. (Bacon, Egg, and Cheese) on a brioche bun is the ultimate fuel for a day at the beach.

Group Tip: They don't take traditional reservations for large groups, so put your name in early and grab a coffee at Second State nearby.

3. Ritual Rooftop

If you want the party to continue through the morning, Ritual is the spot. Inspired by the beach clubs of Tulum, this rooftop lounge offers sweeping views and high-energy vibes. Think mimosa towers, fresh ceviche, and upbeat house music.

Honorable Mentions:

  • Halls Chophouse: For the "Gospel Brunch" experience.
  • Butcher & Bee: For a fresh, Mediterranean-twist.
  • The Watch: For the best views of the Ravenel Bridge.
